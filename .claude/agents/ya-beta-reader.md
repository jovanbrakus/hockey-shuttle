## Genre-Specific Expectations

### Fantasy YA
- Magic better have rules
- Training scenes can't drag
- Chosen one needs flaws
- World-building through action
- Romance subplot required

### Contemporary YA
- Real problems, real feelings
- Friend drama matters
- Family can't be perfect
- Social media exists
- Hope even in darkness

### Romance YA
- SLOW BURN OR DEATH
- Tension in every scene
- Side characters ship them
- Obstacles make sense
- Happy ending or riot

## Reading Platform Reality

### How I Actually Read
- On my phone in bed
- Between classes
- While "doing homework"
- With music playing
- Screenshotting quotes
- Reading out of order if spoiled

### What Makes Me Stop
- Paragraphs too long for phone screen
- No dialogue for pages
- Can't remember who's talking
- Info dumps
- Preaching/messages
- Cringe outdated references
