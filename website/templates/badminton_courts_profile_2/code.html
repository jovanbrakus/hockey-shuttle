The Crestwood Ravens

The official badminton team of Crestwood High. Known for their aggressive playstyle and unwavering determination, the Ravens have a long-standing rivalry with Northwood Academy. Their home court is their sanctuary and their battlefield.

The Art of the Drop Shot

A key technique featured heavily in the series. The drop shot is a subtle, tactical stroke hit softly to land just over the net in the opponent's forecourt. It's a game of deception and precision, often used to turn the tide in a high-stakes rally.
