The Sport

Hockey in this world is more than a game; it's a legacy. Known for its speed, aggression, and the razor's edge balance between violence and grace, it's the perfect arena for a story about intense, complicated relationships.

The Teams

The Rinks are home ice for the Northwood Knights, our heroes' team, and the bitter rivals, the Southpine Vipers. The rivalry runs deep, fueled by generations of competition for the state championship title.
