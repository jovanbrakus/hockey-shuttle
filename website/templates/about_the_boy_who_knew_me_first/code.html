About The Boy Who Knew Me First

An immersive journey into the world of YA sports romance, delivered in a unique episodic format.

Dive into the heart of 'The Boy Who Knew Me First,' a captivating Young Adult sports romance told across ten thrilling episodes. This series explores the intricate dynamics of friendship, rivalry, and first love, set against the competitive backdrops of hockey and badminton. Discover what makes this story unique, from its compelling character arcs to its innovative storytelling format designed for modern readers.

Crafted for fans of heartfelt YA romance and compelling sports drama, this series follows the journey of characters navigating the pressures of competition and the complexities of their relationships. Each episode is a new chapter, available for free in multiple formats, including PDF, ePub, and online.

The story was born from a passion for exploring how shared history and new challenges can shape the course of love, offering a personal glimpse into the creative process and the inspirations behind the series.
