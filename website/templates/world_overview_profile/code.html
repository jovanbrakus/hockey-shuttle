World Overview

Where rivalries ignite, loyalties are tested, and love blossoms against the backdrop of high-stakes sports.

From the roaring crowds of Oakwood High Stadium to the hushed tension of the championship arena in Crestview City, every location holds a piece of their story. This is a world defined by the chalk lines of the football field, the quiet moments of reflection by Miller's Lake, and the bustling energy of downtown streets after a game.

Explore the interconnected map of their lives, where the rivalry with Northwood Academy fuels their ambition and the winding paths of Eagle Ridge Trail offer a secret escape. It is in these places—the locker rooms, the late-night diners, and the sun-drenched bleachers—that destinies are forged and hearts are won and lost.
