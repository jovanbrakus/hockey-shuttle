About Maya

Maya Foster is the fierce captain of the badminton team who cuts through BS with surgical precision. Direct, competitive, and unapologetically herself, Maya evaluates life like a match—strategic, decisive, and always looking for the winning shot. She's the girl who says exactly what she thinks, who respects skill over charm, and who protects the people she cares about with the same intensity she brings to the court. But beneath the confident exterior is someone who's mastered the game everywhere except off the court, someone who's just as uncertain about people as everyone else but hides it behind decisive action. When she tells you the hard truth, it's because she believes you're strong enough to handle it—and that's the highest compliment she knows how to give.
