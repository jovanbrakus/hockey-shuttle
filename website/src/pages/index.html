About the Series

The Boy Who Knew Me First is a 10-episode YA sports romance series that explores identity, pressure, and the complicated beauty of first love. Set against the backdrop of competitive hockey and badminton in Winnipeg, this is a story about finding yourself while everyone else tells you who you should be.

Sophia Chen is brilliant—on the badminton court and in the physics classroom. But when she returns to Winnipeg for senior year, she's the new girl trying to figure out where she belongs. Ethan Price is the star hockey captain everyone expects to go pro, but secretly dreams of studying engineering at MIT. Their childhood friendship was pure and simple. Their reunion is anything but.

Through 10 binge-worthy episodes, watch as they navigate the impossible: maintaining a friendship when it feels like more, pursuing dreams when everyone's watching, and choosing authenticity in a world that rewards performance. This is a slow-burn romance with authentic teen voices, real stakes, and the kind of emotional honesty that makes you feel every moment.
