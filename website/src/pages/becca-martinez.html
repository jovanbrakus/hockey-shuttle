About Becca

Becca Martinez is the student council president who stages life like the perfect photograph—coordinated, curated, controlled. Upbeat and organized, she navigates senior year with color-coded planners and carefully scheduled spontaneity, believing that if everything looks perfect, it will eventually feel perfect too. She's the girl who has her future mapped out, who knows exactly how relationships should work, and who's built her identity around being the perfect girlfriend to the perfect hockey captain. But beneath the polished performance is someone terrified of losing control, someone who's starting to realize that planning every moment doesn't prevent heartbreak. When her carefully constructed perfect senior year begins to dissolve, she'll have to decide whether to hold tighter or finally let herself be real—even if real is messy, uncertain, and nothing like the script she wrote.
