About Sophia

Sophia Chen is brilliant—on the badminton court and in the physics classroom. After seven years away, she returns to Winnipeg as the new girl trying to figure out where she belongs. A gifted badminton player and physics whiz, Sophia sees the world in patterns and equations, constantly analyzing and second-guessing herself. She's the girl who thinks before she speaks, who calculates social interactions like problems to solve. But beneath the analytical exterior is someone who feels everything deeply, who's terrified of not measuring up, and who's discovering that some things—like the way your heart remembers what your mind tried to forget—can't be solved with logic alone.
