## Episode Overview

**Logline:** After admitting her feelings, Sophia tries to integrate into Ethan's world while he struggles between duty to Becca and his growing feelings. When his parents label her "problematic" and she overhears a misunderstood conversation, everything accelerates toward an explosive confrontation that changes everything.

**Timeline:** Late September - Early October (weeks 3-4 of junior year)

## Themes

1. **Appropriate vs. Authentic**
   - Parents want "appropriate friendship"
   - But authenticity demands more
   - Cost of choosing real over right

2. **Performance Under Pressure**
   - Ethan performing for parents, scouts, Becca
   - Sophia performing at tournament while heartbroken
   - When performance cracks, truth emerges

3. **Variables and Equations**
   - Sophia's metaphor throughout
   - "I'm not the variable in his equation"
   - "Trying to be the right variable in someone else's equation"
   - Episode ends: "Tomorrow, she was going to choose herself"

4. **Sacrifice and Investment**
   - His parents' 12 years of sacrifice
   - Their right to influence vs. his autonomy
   - What he owes them vs. what he owes himself

5. **Earned Excellence**
   - Sophia's silver medal proving she's elite too
   - Not just "girl who distracted hockey star"
   - Both excellent at sports AND academics
   - Her achievement separate from him
