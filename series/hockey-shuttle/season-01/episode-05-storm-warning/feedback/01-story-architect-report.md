# STORY-ARCHITECT REPORT: EPISODE 5 "STORM WARNING"

**Overall Grade**: 7.5/10 (B+)

## Executive Summary

Episode 5 successfully delivers as a midseason climax with strong emotional beats and effective pacing through Chapters 1-3. However, Chapter 4 suffers from significant structural issues, particularly a rushed confrontation sequence and missing scenes promised in the outline.

## Critical Structural Issues

### 1. Compressed Climax Sequence (Chapter 4, Pages 73-80)
**Severity**: CRITICAL

The driveway confrontation/removal happens in approximately 8 pages when it needs 12-15 pages for proper impact. The outline promises:
- Public confrontation with all parents present
- Physical removal by Robert
- Neighbors watching, police arriving
- Sophia collapsing, Maya catching her

**Current draft**: Much of this is summarized or absent.

**Recommendation**: Expand by 1,500 words with full scene details.

### 2. Disconnected Epilogue (Chapter 4, Sunday Morning)
**Severity**: IMPORTANT

Robert's Sunday morning visit feels like a different episode. It disrupts the emotional throughline and provides closure when readers should be left devastated.

**Recommendation**: Move this scene to Episode 6 opening.
