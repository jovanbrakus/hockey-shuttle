# EPISODE 4: "MATCHUP"

## Episode Overview

**Timeline**: Late November to mid-December to New Year's Eve (weeks 10-14 of junior year)
**Page Count**: ~80 pages (20,000 words)
**Structure**: 4 chapters × 20 pages each
**Episode Type**: Relationship Complication + Emotional Escalation

### Logline
After discovering his parents' prep school plans, Sophia tries to do the noble thing by dating Tyler Kim—a genuinely perfect guy Ethan introduces her to. But you can't logic your way out of loving someone, and as the forced distance becomes unbearable, they face an impossible truth: they have six weeks until he leaves, and they're wasting them pretending.

### Episode Function in Season Arc
- **Season Position**: Episode 4 of 10 (Pre-midseason)
- **A-Plot**: Sophia's doomed relationship with Tyler Kim
- **B-Plot**: Prep school countdown and college applications
- **C-Plot**: Ethan's hockey season struggles, Sophia's badminton nationals prep
- **Series Arc**: First real kiss, relationship goes public, Robert Price escalates

## Episode 3 Cliffhanger Resolution

**The Cliffhanger**: Jordan revealed Robert and Catherine Price are maneuvering legally—prep school in Michigan starting after Christmas break. "They're going to separate you. And they're going to make it look like it's for his own good."

**Resolution Strategy**:
- Open Chapter 1 immediately after Jordan's revelation
- Sophia's internal debate: walk away to protect Ethan's future
- Week of painful distance as she tries to sacrifice
- Ethan's confusion and hurt at her sudden coldness
- Tyler Kim introduced as "safe" alternative
- Ethan trying to be supportive friend, dying inside

## Four-Chapter Structure

### CHAPTER 1: "The Setup" (~5,000 words, Pages 1-20)

**Purpose**: Cliffhanger resolution + Tyler introduction + impossible choice

**Timeline**: Late November (week after Episode 3)

**Key Scenes**:

1. **Cold Open: The Decision** (Pages 1-3)
   - Night of Jordan's revelation
   - Sophia lying in bed, physics notebook open
   - Working through the "equation": her + Ethan = destroyed future
   - Decision made: walk away for his sake
   - "Sometimes the right answer hurts"

2. **Week of Distance** (Pages 4-8)
   - Monday: Avoiding him in hallways
   - Study group awkward, she sits far away
   - Ethan's confused texts she doesn't answer fully
   - School feeling huge and empty despite being near him
   - Maya: "What are you doing?"
   - "Protecting him."

3. **The Introduction** (Pages 9-13)
   - Thursday lunch, Ethan approaches with teammate
   - "Sophia, this is Tyler Kim. Defense, senior, going to UBC next year."
   - Tyler: friendly, genuine, mentions loving math
   - Ethan: "Tyler's brilliant. You guys should talk calculus."
   - The look in Ethan's eyes—he's trying so hard
   - Tyler asks about her badminton, actually listens

4. **The Ask** (Pages 14-17)
   - Friday after school, Tyler finds her
   - "This is going to sound random, but... winter formal?"
   - "It's not until December, I know. But I wanted to ask before anyone else did."
   - Her hesitation
   - Tyler: "Just as friends if you want. No pressure."
   - She says yes (trying to move on)

5. **The Fallout** (Pages 18-20)
   - Ethan hearing the news (Jordan tells him)
   - His hockey practice that afternoon: vicious, angry
   - Coach benching him for slashing penalty
   - Him sitting alone in locker room after
   - Chapter ends: His phone, her contact name, his thumb hovering over call
   - He doesn't press it
   - **Mini-cliffhanger**: "This is what she wants. This is better for her."

**Character States**:
- **Sophia**: Trying to be noble, feeling like she's suffocating
- **Ethan**: Supportive on outside, breaking on inside
- **Tyler**: Genuinely interested, completely oblivious
- **Maya**: Concerned friend watching disaster unfold
- **Jordan**: Guilty for causing this, trying to help both friends

**Emotional Tone**: Painful nobility, forced smiles, winter settling in like grief

### CHAPTER 2: "The Wrong Equation" (~5,000 words, Pages 21-40)

**Purpose**: Tyler relationship development + showing it's wrong + excruciating proximity torture

**Timeline**: Early December (weeks 11-12)

**Key Scenes**:

1. **First Date** (Pages 21-26)
   - Saturday, December 2nd
   - Coffee shop downtown, Tyler perfect gentleman
   - Conversation flows: math, sports, future plans
   - He's funny, kind, interesting
   - Everything should work
   - Nothing clicks
   - Walking her home, respectful distance
   - "I had a really great time, Sophia."
   - "Me too." (Lie feels heavy)

2. **Study Group Torture** (Pages 27-31)
   - Wednesday evening, Jordan's house
   - Tyler now part of the group
   - Sitting between Tyler and Ethan's empty chair
   - Ethan arrives late, takes seat across from her
   - Tyler's hand on her shoulder (casual, friendly)
   - Ethan's jaw tightening
   - Physics problem she can't solve
   - Ethan: "The variable's wrong. You're using the wrong variable."
   - Eye contact that lasts too long
   - Tyler noticing

3. **Badminton Practice** (Pages 32-35)
   - Thursday, Maya partnering with her
   - Sophia playing distracted, missing easy shots
   - Maya finally: "Okay, what the hell?"
   - "Tyler's great."
   - "That's not an answer to the question I'm asking."
   - "I'm doing the right thing."
   - "For who?"
   - Sophia smashing shuttlecock so hard it splits
   - Coach: "Chen! Whatever's going on, leave it off my court."

4. **The Hockey Game** (Pages 36-38)
   - Friday night, Tyler asked her to come watch
   - Sitting with hockey girlfriends (awkward)
   - Watching Ethan play—angry, reckless
   - Taking penalties, ignoring plays
   - Coach screaming at him
   - Tyler beside her: "He's been off all week. You know what's up?"
   - "No idea."
   - Ethan looking up at stands, seeing her with Tyler
   - Next shift: complete shutdown, plays perfectly
   - Then back to anger

5. **After the Game** (Pages 39-40)
   - Tyler walking her to car
   - "You're a good luck charm. He played better when he saw you."
   - Her heart breaking
   - Tyler's goodnight kiss—gentle, on cheek
   - Feeling nothing
   - Driving home, crying in parking lot
   - **Mini-cliffhanger**: Text from Ethan: "We need to talk. Please."

**Character States**:
- **Sophia**: Increasingly desperate, failing at pretending
- **Ethan**: Angry hockey, barely controlled pain
- **Tyler**: Starting to notice something's off
- **Maya**: Frustrated with best friend's martyrdom

**Emotional Tone**: Wrong, wrong, wrong—like wearing shoes on wrong feet, constant discomfort

### CHAPTER 3: "Complications" (~5,000 words, Pages 41-60)

**Purpose**: Breaking point + Tyler's grace + Christmas longing + college apps pressure

**Timeline**: Mid-December (week 13)

**Key Scenes**:

1. **College Application Crunch** (Pages 41-45)
   - December 15th, deadlines approaching
   - Common App essay: "Describe a time you faced an ethical dilemma"
   - Writing about authenticity while lying to Tyler
   - Her essays hollow, forcing words
   - MIT, Caltech, Cornell, Princeton
   - All asking "What matters to you?"
   - Not being able to write "Him" anywhere

2. **Winter Formal** (Pages 46-52)
   - Saturday, December 16th
   - Hotel ballroom, Christmas lights, snow outside
   - Tyler in suit, her in blue dress (Maya helped shop)
   - He's attentive, sweet, trying so hard
   - Dancing feeling mechanical
   - Across the gym: Ethan with random date (Sarah Something)
   - Both couples dancing
   - Both of them watching each other
   - DJ plays slow song
   - Tyler holding her close: "You okay? You seem far away."
   - "Just tired."
   - Ethan and Sarah dancing closer
   - Her eyes meeting Ethan's over Tyler's shoulder
   - Everything else disappearing
   - Song ending, spell breaking

3. **The Parking Lot** (Pages 53-56)
   - After formal, Tyler getting coats
   - Sophia outside getting air
   - Ethan coming out (Sarah went to bathroom)
   - "Nice dress."
   - "Thanks."
   - Silence, snow falling
   - "Sophie—"
   - "Don't."
   - "This isn't working."
   - "It has to."
   - "Why?"
   - "Because you're leaving. Because your parents—"
   - "So you're dating someone you don't care about? That's the solution?"
   - "I care about Tyler."
   - "But you don't love him."
   - Silence is admission
   - Tyler calling her name from door
   - Ethan stepping back
   - "Have a good night, Sophia."

4. **Tyler Overhears** (Pages 57-59)
   - Next Wednesday, study group
   - Tyler arriving early, hearing voices
   - Sophia and Ethan in kitchen
   - "I can't keep doing this."
   - "Then don't. Break up with him."
   - "And then what? You leave in two weeks anyway."
   - "At least we'd have two weeks."
   - "That makes it worse!"
   - Tyler in doorway: "Hey."
   - Both jumping apart
   - The knowing look in Tyler's eyes

5. **The Conversation** (Pages 60)
   - Tyler asking to talk after study group
   - His car, honest and kind
   - "You're in love with someone else."
   - "Tyler—"
   - "It's okay. I mean, it sucks, but it's okay."
   - "You're such a good person."
   - "Just not the right person. For you, anyway."
   - "I'm sorry."
   - "Don't be. Just... be honest. With yourself and with him."
   - "It's complicated."
   - "It always is. But lying doesn't make it simpler."
   - **Mini-cliffhanger**: Christmas Eve, midnight
   - Her phone: "Merry Christmas, Watson. Miss you."
   - Her thumb hovering over reply
   - Finally: "Miss you too, Sherlock."

**Character States**:
- **Sophia**: Breaking point, can't maintain facade
- **Ethan**: Done respecting her choice, fighting now
- **Tyler**: Perceptive and gracious, lets her go
- **Everyone**: Feeling time pressure (prep school looming)

**Emotional Tone**: Shattering pretense, painful honesty, winter loneliness

### CHAPTER 4: "New Year's Resolution" (~5,000 words, Pages 61-80)

**Purpose**: Breaking up with Tyler + New Year's party + FIRST KISS + Major cliffhanger

**Timeline**: December 28-January 2

**Key Scenes**:

1. **Official Breakup** (Pages 61-64)
   - December 28th, coffee shop
   - Sophia asking Tyler to meet
   - "I can't do this anymore. You deserve someone who's all in."
   - "And you deserve to stop punishing yourself."
   - "He's leaving for prep school. Tomorrow."
   - "Then you have today."
   - Hug goodbye, genuine friendship
   - Tyler: "For what it's worth? He looks at you the same way."

2. **New Year's Eve Party Setup** (Pages 65-68)
   - December 31st, Maya's house
   - Parents out of town, responsible party
   - Sophia arriving with Maya and Jordan
   - Everyone talking about resolutions
   - Jordan: "Mine's to stop watching my friends be idiots."
   - Sophia: "Helpful."
   - 11 PM, still no Ethan
   - Maya: "He's coming. He texted."
   - Sophia trying not to watch door

3. **The Balcony** (Pages 69-74)
   - 11:30 PM, finding Ethan outside
   - Winnipeg winter, -20°C, stars brilliant
   - Him in just sweater, she brings coat
   - "You'll freeze."
   - "Seems fitting."
   - "I broke up with Tyler."
   - "I know. He told me."
   - "You talked to him?"
   - "He called. Said I was an idiot if I didn't fight for you."
   - "Tyler said that?"
   - "He's a good guy."
   - "Just not my guy."
   - Silence, snow falling
   - "I leave for Michigan in four days."
   - "I know."
   - "So we'd have six weeks. That's it. Spring break, then I'm back there."
   - "I know."
   - "And it's going to hurt. When I leave, it's going to destroy us."
   - "I know."
   - "So why would we do this?"
   - Her turning to face him fully
   - "Because I'm tired of pretending I don't love you."

4. **Countdown to Midnight** (Pages 75-78)
   - Inside, party counting down
   - Them on balcony, hearing numbers
   - "Sophie—"
   - "Don't think. For once, just don't think."
   - "That's supposed to be my line."
   - "10... 9... 8..."
   - His hand on her face
   - "7... 6... 5..."
   - Her hand on his chest, his heart racing
   - "4... 3... 2..."
   - "I love you too. So much it scares me."
   - "1... HAPPY NEW YEAR!"
   - The kiss—finally, real, right
   - Fireworks somewhere in distance
   - Party noisemakers inside
   - Them in their own world
   - Breaking apart, foreheads touching
   - "So what now?"
   - "Now we have six weeks. Let's not waste them."

5. **The Cliffhanger** (Pages 79-80)
   - January 2nd, 10 AM
   - Sophia home, helping mom with decorations
   - Black Mercedes pulling into driveway
   - Robert Price getting out—alone
   - Envelope in his hand
   - Her stomach dropping
   - Dad answering door
   - "Mr. Chen. I apologize for the intrusion. But we need to discuss your daughter's relationship with my son."
   - Dad: "I think you should come in."
   - Sophia frozen in kitchen
   - Robert Price in their living room
   - Envelope on coffee table
   - **EPISODE CLIFFHANGER**:
   - Robert: "I've prepared an offer. One I think you'll find... persuasive."
   - Opening envelope, pulling out documents
   - "A full scholarship to MIT. Guaranteed admission, all expenses paid."
   - Her confusion: "What?"
   - "All you have to do is agree to stop seeing Ethan. Completely. No contact until after college."
   - "You're bribing me to break up with your son?"
   - Robert's cold smile: "I'm investing in your future, Sophia. And protecting his."
   - "Where's Ethan?"
   - "He doesn't know I'm here. And he won't. Unless you make me tell him."
   - The envelope sitting there
   - Everything she's worked for, right there
   - Everything she wants, requiring she give up everything else
   - **FINAL LINE**: "You have 24 hours to decide."
