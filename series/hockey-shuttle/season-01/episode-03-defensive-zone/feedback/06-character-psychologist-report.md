**What's Shown:**
- Financial coercion
- Phone surveillance  
- Forced isolation
- Legal documentation threat
- "Investment" language

**The Problem:** Narrative acknowledges "controlling" but doesn't explicitly name it as **abuse**

**Reader Impact:** Young readers in similar situations may not recognize this as abusive

**Fix Required:** Have character say: "This isn't normal parenting. This is abuse."
