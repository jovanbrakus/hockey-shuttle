# DIALOGUE-COACH REPORT: Episode 3

## Overall Grade: B+
**Strong voices with room for naturalistic polish**

## Critical Issues:

### 1. Over-Articulation in Emotional Moments

**Ch1, Line 171-177:** Too composed for teenager who just got ultimatum
```
Current: "They see their investment at risk. They see me potentially not making 
the NHL because I'm 'distracted by a relationship.'"

Better: "They don't— They think I'm throwing it all away. Like you're some kind of... 
They actually said 'distraction.' They called you a distraction."
```

### 2. Physics Metaphors - OVERUSED

Sophia uses them constantly (6+ times):
- Line 29: "retreat into equations"
- Line 305: "particles with charge"
- Line 399: "quantum mechanics"
- Line 402: "Heisenberg uncertainty"

**Fix:** Vary her analytical expressions OR have friends call this out more

### 3. Missing Natural Teen Speech Elements

❌ Very few filler words ("like," "um," "I mean")
❌ Too few incomplete thoughts
❌ Everyone finishes sentences perfectly when stressed

**Fix:** Add conversational messiness selectively
