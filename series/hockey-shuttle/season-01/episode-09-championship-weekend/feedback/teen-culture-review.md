## Executive Summary

Episode 9 has **exceptional emotional authenticity** and **strong character development**, but the dialogue and behavior patterns skew too mature and formal. The teens sound like articulate adults discussing their feelings rather than actual 17-18 year olds navigating high-stakes situations. Technology integration feels surface-level, contemporary slang is nearly absent, and some behaviors are unrealistically composed for teenagers under extreme stress.

**Strengths:**
- Emotional realism (anxiety, exhaustion, relief)
- Family dynamics feel genuine
- Friend support systems ring true
- Physical manifestations of stress (can't eat, shaking hands, crying)

**Weaknesses:**
- Dialogue too polished and formal
- Limited contemporary teen speech patterns
- Technology present but not organically integrated
- Missing casual communication styles
- Some behavior unrealistically mature
- Dated or missing cultural touchpoints
