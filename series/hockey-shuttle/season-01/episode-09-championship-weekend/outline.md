## Episode Synopsis

The most important day of their lives. May 15th. Two hearings that will determine everything: criminal charges at 9 AM, emancipation at 10 AM. If Sophia doesn't drop Ethan by 9 AM, prosecution proceeds. If emancipation fails at 10 AM, he remains under Robert's control until he turns 18 (August 12th - three more months of hell).

This episode is ONE INTENSE DAY chronicling every hour from 6 AM wake-up through midnight celebration. Legal battles, parent confrontations, judge decisions, protesters, media, paperwork, and finally - FINALLY - when emancipation is granted and charges are dropped, they get their real first kiss as truly free people.

The episode ends with celebration, relief, and the knowledge that they've won their freedom - but then a phone call introduces a third option they never considered: an NHL contract that would give Ethan both hockey AND MIT. Everything they sacrificed for, now available with millions of dollars attached. Did they choose right?
