**Issues:**

- Too articulate when stressed
- Missing teen speech patterns

**Solutions:**

**Replace therapy-speak with teen talk:**
- "processing" → "I need to think" or "my brain is melting"
- "generational money" → "stupid amounts of money"
- "emotional autonomy" → "figuring my shit out without him"

**Add filler words when stressed:**

- Incomplete sentences when emotional

- Topic jumping in casual conversations
- Inside jokes we don't fully understand
- Occasional profanity when adults aren't around
