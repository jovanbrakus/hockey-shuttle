- Most jurisdictions have close-in-age exemptions

- Requires adult encouraging **criminal** behavior
- Is "truancy" (missing school) criminal in this jurisdiction?
- Were they actually truant or just meeting after school?

**Interference with Custody**:
- Usually requires **third party** preventing parent access
- Does teenage girlfriend count as "interfering"?
- This charge typically for kidnapping/hiding child

**Stalking/Harassment**:
- Requires **unwanted** contact
- Ethan clearly wants contact
- Parent setting boundaries ‚â† harassment by girlfriend

### The Issue:

These charges feel **legally questionable** but the episode doesn't acknowledge this:
- No lawyer saying "these might not stick"
- No discussion of Romeo and Juliet laws
- Presented as **definitely prosecutable**
