## The Obstacle: Becca

**Not a villain - a complication:**
- Genuinely nice person
- Loves Ethan (or loves the idea of him)
- Student council president, has her life together
- Fits perfectly into his world
- Doesn't see beneath his performance
- Getting anxious about Sophia without being obvious
- "Relationship goals" according to everyone

**The Real Issue:**
- Ethan is with her but opening up to Sophia
- Sophia respects relationships but is falling anyway
- Becca represents the easy path (hockey, popularity, expectations)
- Sophia represents the harder path (authenticity, questions, change)

3. **Balanced POV:** Deep in Sophia's head but Ethan's feelings evident
4. **Sensory Details:** Ice, cold, hockey sounds, badminton speed - all vivid
5. **Authentic Dialogue:** Teens text, use casual speech, avoid melodrama
