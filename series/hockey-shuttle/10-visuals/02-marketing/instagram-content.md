# The Boy Who Knew Me First - Instagram Marketing Visuals

## Overview
Instagram content for promoting The Boy Who Knew Me First should balance aesthetic appeal, authenticity, and strategic book marketing. Content types include:
- Grid posts (permanent feed)
- Stories (24-hour, more casual)
- Reels (short video format - prompts for static images that suggest motion)
- Carousel posts (multiple images)

**Target Audience**:
- YA readers (ages 15-25)
- Bookstagram community
- Romance readers
- Sports fiction fans
- Wattpad readers

### Posting Frequency
- **Pre-launch**: 3-5 posts per week building anticipation
- **Launch week**: Daily posts + stories

## Final Instagram Strategy Notes

**Authenticity Over Perfection**: YA readers value genuine over polished
**Community Over Promotion**: Engage more than you post
**Value Over Ads**: Every post should offer something (entertainment, emotion, info)
**Consistency Over Intensity**: Regular posts beat sporadic bursts
**Patience**: Instagram book marketing is a slow build, like the romance itself

The goal isn't viral fame - it's building a community of invested readers who will follow the series through multiple seasons.
