## Usage Notes

### Image Generation Settings
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 for atmospheric establishing shots
- **Resolution:** 8K quality for professional finish
- **Style:** Cinematic photography, editorial quality
- **Mood:** Contemporary YA romance aesthetic

### Series Branding Consistency
- Spring City should feel **aspirational but achievable**
- Balance between **urban sophistication and teen accessibility**
- Consistent color palette: warm golds, twilight blues, spring greens, soft pinks
- Lighting should be **natural and cinematic** (golden hour, blue hour)
- Always include **subtle sports/academic references** in environment

### Scene Applications
1. **Street Scene**: Episode openings, establishing shots, daily life montages
2. **Café/Boardwalk**: Reunion scenes, conversation moments, neutral meeting ground
3. **Evening Waterfront**: Romantic tension scenes, season finale moments, emotional peaks

### Variation Ideas
- **Morning version**: Replace golden hour with fresh morning light and fog
- **Winter version**: Add snow, holiday lights, colder color palette
- **Rainy version**: Wet streets with reflections, umbrella moments
- **Event version**: Add food truck festival, outdoor market, hockey parade

---

## Creative Direction Notes

### Spring City Character
Spring City should feel like:
- A place where **ambitions are supported** (sports excellence, academic achievement)
- **Accessible urban environment** (teens can navigate independently)
- **Balance of energy and intimacy** (public spaces with private moments)
- **Seasonal beauty** (cherry blossoms, fall colors, winter snow, summer green)
- **Modern but not intimidating** (contemporary design, human scale)

### Photography Style Reference
- Contemporary urban photography
- Cinematic film color grading
- Natural lighting with dramatic timing
- Shallow depth of field for intimacy
- Wide angles for establishing context
- Bokeh and light play for romance

### Color Theory Application
- **Warm tones** (golden hour, café lights): Comfort, connection, nostalgia
- **Cool tones** (twilight, water): Longing, possibility, calm
- **Spring palette** (pinks, greens): New beginnings, youth, growth
- **Urban neutrals** (grays, whites): Modern, achievable, real
